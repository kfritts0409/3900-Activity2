Shimmer

Foundation Bred Appaloosa



My dear Shimmer. Shimmer was my first Foundation Bred Appaloosa and will always be missed. I lost Shimmer in 2021 after a long year of battling some unknown neurological issue. She was in every aspect my dream mare, and I was hoping she still be here with me to help my Appaloosa journey grow. However, I got to own her for four incredible years and cherish every moment. Artemis has some very big shoes to fill, but she fills them well. I wouldn’t expect anything less, as she is the granddaughter of Shimmer. Shimmer subtly shows off her Appaloosa characteristics, but they are there! A fun fact, on her dam (mother’s side), a Secretariat is her 4th grandsire.
